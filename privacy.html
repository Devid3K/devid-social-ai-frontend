Privacy Policy

Devid Social AI — Social Media AI Content Creation Platform

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect: email, name (registration), social account metadata (platform, follower count), and AI-generated content (scripts, videos).

2. TikTok Data

We use TikTok Research API to access aggregate, public trend data only: trending hashtags, video performance metrics, and product trends. We do NOT collect or store individual TikTok user data, private videos, or personal information.

3. How We Use Data

Data is used solely for: trend analysis, content recommendation, viral score calculation, and helping creators optimize their social media strategy.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored on secure servers. Financial data uses encrypted storage. Users can delete their account and all associated data at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with: TikTok Research API, Groq AI, Google Gemini, SerpAPI. Each service has its own privacy policy.
